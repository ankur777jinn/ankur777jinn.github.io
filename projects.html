Genetic Pruning

Evolutionary pruning framework for large language models. Treats subnetworks as organisms in a population, evolving compact architectures through selection, crossover, and mutation. The codebase behind our AAAI 2026 Student Abstract on "Natural Selection of LLMs via Pruning."

Sycophancy & Sandbagging Aren't That Different

An investigation into whether sycophancy and sandbagging share mechanistic roots inside transformer models. If they're mechanistically distinct, safety researchers need separate classifiers and steering vectors for every failure mode — if they're not, a unified intervention might suffice.
